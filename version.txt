HMaker version: 2.2.0 (Betta)
New in realise:
    now syntax was changed:
    HMaker [-s -r --version --update --path=path/to/header.h] {arg1, arg2, arg3 e.t.c.}

Format:
    You can give to HMaker many arguments and it's may be DIRECTORIES, FILE.C or FILE.H
    For choose headername write args like : "file.c=headername.h file.h=headername.h som/dir=headername.h"

    --path={headername.h} - all data will be put in ONE header named "headername.h"
    -r - recursive parsing (works ONLY with --path)
